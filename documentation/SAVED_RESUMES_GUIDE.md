# How to View Your Saved Resumes

## Overview
You can now view all your saved resumes through the **Resume Workspace**. There are two main ways to view your resumes:

## Method 1: Resume Versions Tab (Recommended)

1. Navigate to the **Resume Workspace** (Resumes page)
2. Click the **"Resume Versions"** tab at the top
3. You'll see a two-panel layout:
   - **Left panel**: List of all your saved resumes with titles and dates
   - **Right panel**: Professional resume preview

### How to use:
- Click on any resume title in the left panel
- The resume will load and display in a professional chronological format on the right
- The selected resume is highlighted in blue
- Use the scrollbar to view the complete resume

## Method 2: My Resumes Dashboard

1. Navigate to the **Resume Workspace** (Resumes page)
2. Stay on the **"Dashboard"** tab (default)
3. You'll see your resumes as cards showing:
   - Resume title
   - Last updated date
   - Two buttons: **Preview** and **Edit**

### How to use:
- Click the **Preview** button to open a full-screen view of the resume
- Click the **Edit** button to edit the resume in the AI Resume Generator
- The preview shows the same professional formatting as the Versions tab

## What You'll See in the Preview

The resume preview displays:
- **Header**: Your name, email, and contact information
- **Professional Summary**: Your career overview
- **Skills**: Organized technical, soft, and domain-specific skills
- **Experience**: Work history with responsibilities and achievements
- **Education**: Degrees and certifications
- **Projects**: Notable projects and accomplishments
- **Certifications**: Professional certifications and training

## Features

✅ **Chronological Layout**: Emphasizes work experience and timeline  
✅ **Professional Formatting**: PDF-like appearance with proper spacing and hierarchy  
✅ **Easy Navigation**: Click-and-view interface with no extra steps  
✅ **Scrollable Preview**: View complete resumes even on smaller screens  

## Technical Details

- Resumes are fetched from the backend with your authentication token
- The preview uses the AI-generated content stored in your `aiContent` field
- All resume data (skills, experience, education, projects) is included
- The preview refreshes automatically when you switch between resumes

## Troubleshooting

**Issue**: No resumes are showing
- Make sure you're logged in and your token is valid
- Try refreshing the page
- Check that you've created/saved resumes in the AI Resume Generator

**Issue**: Preview doesn't load
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure your authentication token is still valid
- Try selecting a different resume
- Check browser console (F12) for error messages

**Issue**: Resume content appears blank
- This might indicate the resume data is incomplete
- Try editing the resume in the AI Resume Generator
- Check if the resume was created with AI generation or uploaded
